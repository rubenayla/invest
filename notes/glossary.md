# Financial Glossary

Quick reference for acronyms and jargon used across these notes.

## Valuation & Models

| Term | Meaning |
|------|---------|
| **DCF** | Discounted Cash Flow — values a company by projecting future cash flows and discounting them to present value |
| **RIM** | Residual Income Model — values equity as book value + present value of future excess returns |
| **WACC** | Weighted Average Cost of Capital — blended cost of debt + equity used as DCF discount rate |
| **NPV** | Net Present Value — sum of discounted future cash flows minus initial investment |
| **GBM** | Gradient Boosted Machine — ML model used here for stock return predictions |
| **LSTM** | Long Short-Term Memory — type of neural network for sequential data |
| **CAGR** | Compound Annual Growth Rate — smoothed annualized return over a period |
| **TAM** | Total Addressable Market — maximum possible revenue for a product |

## Financial Metrics

| Term | Meaning |
|------|---------|
| **EPS** | Earnings Per Share — net income / shares outstanding |
| **P/E** | Price-to-Earnings ratio — stock price / EPS |
| **P/B** | Price-to-Book ratio — market cap / book value of equity |
| **P/S** | Price-to-Sales ratio — market cap / revenue |
| **P/FCF** | Price-to-Free-Cash-Flow ratio |
| **P/TBV** | Price-to-Tangible-Book-Value |
| **EV** | Enterprise Value — market cap + debt - cash (what it costs to buy the whole business) |
| **EV/EBITDA** | Enterprise Value / EBITDA — most common operating valuation multiple |
| **D/E** | Debt-to-Equity ratio — total debt / shareholders' equity |
| **ROE** | Return on Equity — net income / shareholders' equity |
| **ROA** | Return on Assets — net income / total assets |
| **ROI** | Return on Investment |
| **ROIC** | Return on Invested Capital — how efficiently a company uses all capital |
| **TTM** | Trailing Twelve Months — last 4 quarters combined |

## Income Statement

| Term | Meaning |
|------|---------|
| **COGS** | Cost of Goods Sold — direct costs to produce what was sold |
| **EBIT** | Earnings Before Interest & Taxes — operating profit |
| **EBITDA** | Earnings Before Interest, Taxes, Depreciation & Amortization — proxy for operating cash generation |
| **SG&A** | Selling, General & Administrative expenses — overhead costs |
| **R&D** | Research & Development spending |
| **D&A** | Depreciation & Amortization |
| **GAAP** | Generally Accepted Accounting Principles — standard US accounting rules |
| **TAC** | Traffic Acquisition Costs — what Google pays partners (Apple, etc.) for search traffic |

## Cash Flow & Balance Sheet

| Term | Meaning |
|------|---------|
| **FCF** | Free Cash Flow — operating cash flow minus capital expenditures; cash available to shareholders |
| **OCF** | Operating Cash Flow — cash generated from core business operations |
| **CAPEX** | Capital Expenditures — spending on long-term assets (factories, servers, etc.) |
| **FY** | Fiscal Year — a company's accounting year (may not align with calendar year) |

## Corporate Actions & Strategy

| Term | Meaning |
|------|---------|
| **M&A** | Mergers & Acquisitions — buying or combining with other companies |
| **IPO** | Initial Public Offering — first time a company sells stock publicly |
| **SPAC** | Special Purpose Acquisition Company — blank-check company that merges with a private target |
| **JV** | Joint Venture — partnership between two companies for a specific project |
| **B2B** | Business-to-Business sales |
| **OEM** | Original Equipment Manufacturer — company whose products are used as components by others |
| **ESG** | Environmental, Social & Governance — sustainability framework for investing |

## Debt & Rates

| Term | Meaning |
|------|---------|
| **SOFR** | Secured Overnight Financing Rate — benchmark interest rate (replaced LIBOR) |
| **bps** | Basis points — 1/100 of a percentage point (100bps = 1%) |
| **CB** | Convertible Bond — debt that can convert into equity at a set price |

## Trading & Markets

| Term | Meaning |
|------|---------|
| **SI** | Short Interest — percentage of shares sold short |
| **DCA** | Dollar-Cost Averaging — investing fixed amounts at regular intervals |
| **ADR** | American Depositary Receipt — US-listed share of a foreign company |
| **ETF** | Exchange-Traded Fund — basket of securities that trades like a stock |
| **VIX** | CBOE Volatility Index — "fear gauge" measuring expected S&P 500 volatility |
| **YTD** | Year-to-Date |
| **EOD** | End of Day |
| **TA** | Technical Analysis — using price/volume charts to predict moves |

## Energy & Solar (SEDG context)

| Term | Meaning |
|------|---------|
| **LCOE** | Levelized Cost of Energy — total lifetime cost per unit of energy produced |
| **PPA** | Power Purchase Agreement — long-term contract to buy electricity at a fixed price |
| **GW / MW** | Gigawatt / Megawatt — units of power capacity |
| **EPC** | Engineering, Procurement & Construction — turnkey project delivery |
| **ERCOT** | Electric Reliability Council of Texas — Texas grid operator |

## Mining & Commodities

| Term | Meaning |
|------|---------|
| **AISC** | All-In Sustaining Cost — total cost to produce an ounce of gold/silver |
| **LNG** | Liquefied Natural Gas |
| **COMEX** | Commodity Exchange — metals futures market |
| **FX** | Foreign Exchange — currency trading |

## Regulatory & Data Sources

| Term | Meaning |
|------|---------|
| **SEC** | Securities and Exchange Commission — US financial regulator |
| **DOJ** | Department of Justice |
| **DMA** | Digital Markets Act — EU tech regulation |
| **EDGAR** | SEC's Electronic Data Gathering system — where companies file reports |
| **XBRL** | eXtensible Business Reporting Language — structured financial data format |

## Tech / AI

| Term | Meaning |
|------|---------|
| **LLM** | Large Language Model |
| **TPU** | Tensor Processing Unit — Google's custom AI chip |
| **FSD** | Full Self-Driving — Tesla's autonomous driving system |
| **BNPL** | Buy Now, Pay Later — fintech payment model |

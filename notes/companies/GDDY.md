**Sector:** Technology | **Industry:** Software — Infrastructure

## Situation Summary
GDDY is down ~55% from its March 2025 peak of ~$178, absorbing a multi-quarter de-rating after Feb 2026 Q4 print where 2026 revenue guide of $5.2–5.3B implied growth slowing below 7%. The culprit: a $4.99 promotional .com offer that compresses upfront bookings optics even though per-customer economics and attach rates are strong. Underlying cash engine is humming — FY25 FCF $1.58B (+25% YoY), normalized EBITDA margins at 32% with +150 bps expansion guided for 2026, and $1.6B of buybacks retired ~5% of shares. A Website Builder "Not Secure" incident (Apr 13, 2026) adds sentiment pressure. Narrative pivots on whether Airo.ai (25 live agents, "high teens" cohort spend lift, ~30% faster second-product attach) is a real growth reaccelerant or a narrative chip management plays each quarter.

## Variant Perception
- **Consensus view:** Mature domains registrar decelerating to <7% growth; commerce/website builder permanently behind Shopify/Wix; Airo is incrementalism not transformation. Sell-side is still nominally "buy" (avg target $117) but the stock trades at 7x forward EPS — the market does not believe forward estimates. Post-drawdown it is now an *uncrowded* name.
- **Our view:** The 2026 optical deceleration is mostly bookings-mix distortion from promotional pricing, NOT underlying demand collapse. FCF conversion, margin expansion, and buyback velocity remain intact. At $11.1B mcap and $1.58B FCF, **14% FCF yield** with a $3B repurchase authorization running through 2027 creates a mechanical ~9-12%/yr EPS tailwind even if revenue flatlines. However — this is CLOSER TO CONSENSUS "BUY" than variant perception. Sell-side already sees +43% upside. The edge here is behavioral: post-55% drawdown + "guidance scare" leaves a high-quality FCF compounder uncrowded at trough multiples. That's a setup edge, not an information edge.
- **Trigger:** Q1 2026 earnings (late April / early May 2026) — if A&C segment posts low-double-digit growth as guided and Airo monetization metrics accelerate, the "deceleration" narrative breaks. Secondary: ongoing $3B buyback print (mechanical share count reduction visible in every quarterly release).

## Inflection Point
**Mild inflection, not dramatic.** Not a turnaround. The inflection is: (a) stock has de-rated from ~20x FCF to 7x FCF on guidance concerns that are largely optical; (b) buyback velocity is at all-time high (14% of mcap/year); (c) Airo entering monetization phase with early-but-real cohort metrics. Observable evidence of recovery is present — but narrative needs a Q1 print to confirm. This is "buy a proven cash machine at a trough multiple," not "catch a turnaround."

## Bull Case
- 2026 optical deceleration is bookings-mix noise; underlying demand and ARPU continue growing HSD; Q1/Q2 prints restore confidence → re-rate to 12-14x forward EPS (vs 7x today).
- $3B buyback through 2027 retires ~25-27% of remaining shares at sub-$85 prices → mechanical EPS growth of 9-12%/yr compounding on top of operational growth.
- A&C (13% growth, higher-margin) becomes bigger revenue mix, pulling consolidated margins higher — 32% EBITDA margin guide is conservative.
- Airo.ai delivers genuine attach-rate lift (already +30% velocity on 2nd product) → ARPU step-function, not flatline.

## Bear Case
- Promotional .com pricing is not a one-time optics shift but a competitive response to commoditization — margin compression follows in 2027-28.
- SMB commerce share permanently lost to Shopify; website builder permanently lost to Wix/Squarespace. GoDaddy becomes "the registrar" with no growth optionality.
- AI-driven website creation (ChatGPT, Claude artifacts, framework tools) disintermediates the entire website-builder category within 3-5 years.
- Debt refinancing at higher rates compresses FCF; buyback velocity slows; re-rate fails to happen; stock dead money at 8-10x FCF indefinitely.

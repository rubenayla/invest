## Risks

### 1. Oil Price Sensitivity (MEDIUM)
- Revenue tied to drilling activity
- If oil drops to $60-70, E&P cuts spending
- Services pricing compressed in downturns

### 2. Cyclicality (MEDIUM-HIGH)
- Very cyclical business
- E&P capex cuts = immediate revenue hit
- Long recovery cycles

### 3. Competition (MEDIUM)
- Schlumberger (SLB) is larger
- Baker Hughes (BKR) aggressive on tech
- Regional players in shale

### 4. Energy Transition (LOW-MEDIUM)
- Long-term threat from renewables
- But oil services needed for decades
- Diversifying into carbon capture, geothermal

### 5. Debt (MEDIUM)
- 84% debt-to-equity (manageable)
- Needs to maintain cash flow for debt service

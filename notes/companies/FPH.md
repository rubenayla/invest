**Business**: CA master-planned communities; revenue is driven by land sales timing.
**Fiscal year end**: Dec 31

## 3-Case Valuation Framework

**Assumptions**
- Shares: ~70.97M
- Net debt: ~$95M
- Multiples use a blend of P/E and EV/FCF for the same scenario.

### Bear
- Revenue: $180M
- Net margin: 12%
- FCF margin: 18%
- Multiples: P/E 8x, EV/FCF 6x
- Implied value: **~$1.4–$2.4/share**

### Base
- Revenue: $230M
- Net margin: 20%
- FCF margin: 25%
- Multiples: P/E 10x, EV/FCF 8x

### Bull

- Net margin: 24%
- FCF margin: 32%

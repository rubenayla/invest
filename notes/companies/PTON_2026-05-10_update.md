**User position:** ~$3,940 / 8% portfolio (IBKR flat, Revolut -13.6%)

## Verdict: HOLD (lean BUY MORE on pullback to $4.80-$5.00)

Q3 FY2026 (May 7) was the inflection quarter the prior note flagged as the litmus test — and it cleared the bar. Revenue grew YoY for the first time in years, FCF accelerated, GAAP profit printed, and full-year guidance was raised. Thesis is working. But the stock has already moved +24% since the last note and the easy 4.6x→6x EV/EBITDA re-rate is largely captured. Adding aggressively here means paying for what the prior thesis predicted; the asymmetric setup is thinner. Hold core, add only on a flush.

## 3 Key Findings Since Prior Note

1. **Revenue inflection confirmed.** Q3 revenue $630.9M (+1% YoY) vs guidance $605-625M — the first positive YoY print in many quarters. Beat on hardware AND subscription. The "is the turnaround real" question is now answered — yes on profitability AND revenue.
2. **FY26 guidance raised meaningfully.** FCF guide $350M (up from $275M, +$75M), revenue $2.42-2.44B, Adj EBITDA $470-480M. Management now expects positive net income AND positive operating income for full FY2026 — both firsts in company history. Net debt down 70% YoY to $173M.
3. **Subscriber bleed persists.** Paid CF subs 2.662M, -7.6% YoY. Monthly churn still elevated. The profitability inflection is real; the sub-growth inflection is not yet here. Stern's strategic optionality framing is credible but unproven on the top of the funnel.

## Updated Bull / Bear

**Bull**
- First-ever GAAP profitable year in FY26 forces a multi-year re-rating; EV/EBITDA could expand from ~5x toward 7-9x as turnaround risk premium compresses. FCF yield ~14% on a now-deleveraging balance sheet is rare.
- Commercial Series (ships late 2026) and app-only subs are upside optionality the market hasn't priced in; Einhorn's $7.50-31.50 range still intact and now more credible.

**Bear**
- Subscriber base still shrinking 7-8%/yr — eventually EBITDA peaks if hardware/commercial doesn't replace lost subs. "Shrinking to profitability" has a finite runway.
- Stock is now $5.70 with 52-week high $9.20; easy gains made. Macro/consumer-discretionary shock or a single soft quarter could retrace 25-30%. Position is already 8% of portfolio — sizing risk is real.

# SoftBank Group Corp (9984.T)

**Sector:** Communication Services | **Industry:** Telecom / AI Investment Holding
**Price:** JPY 3,507 | **Market Cap:** JPY 20.0T (~$133B)
**Analysis Date:** 2026-03-23

## Situation Summary

SoftBank Group is at its lowest since August 2025, down ~49% from its 52-week high of JPY 6,924. The selloff intensified in March after the Stargate AI data center project hit serious setbacks — partner disputes between OpenAI, Oracle, and SoftBank over governance and financing led to the abandoned Texas expansion. S&P revised SoftBank's credit outlook to negative after a $30B follow-on OpenAI investment (cumulative $64.6B, ~13% stake), and PayPay's US IPO priced below range. CEO Son has declared "total offense mode," pivoting SoftBank into a concentrated AI holding company (90% Arm + OpenAI + Izanagi custom silicon). The market is punishing the concentration risk, leverage, and execution uncertainty. NAV per share is estimated at ~JPY 19,600, meaning the stock trades at an ~82% discount to NAV.

## Variant Perception

- **Consensus view:** SoftBank is overleveraged and over-concentrated in speculative AI bets. Stargate may stall, OpenAI's valuation could compress, and Arm faces cyclical headwinds if AI capex slows. S&P negative outlook signals potential downgrades. Son is repeating Vision Fund 1 mistakes — betting too big, too fast.
- **Our view:** The 82% NAV discount is pricing in catastrophic failure across all major holdings simultaneously. Yet the core assets have real, growing value: Arm's data center royalties are growing 100%+ YoY and will soon surpass mobile; OpenAI (13% stake at ~$300B valuation = ~$39B) continues rapid revenue scaling; the telecom subsidiary generates stable JPY 1T+ annual EBITDA. Even under conservative Arm valuations (40x PE), SoftBank's proportional Arm stake (~$90B) plus OpenAI (~$39B) plus telecom (~$26B) totals ~$155B vs $133B market cap — meaning the market assigns negative value to Vision Fund 2, Izanagi, and all other assets. The NAV discount at 82% is historically extreme and unsustainable if even one major asset delivers.
- **Trigger:** (1) Q4 FY2025 earnings May 7, 2026 — NAV update and Stargate commentary. (2) Any Stargate dispute resolution. (3) Izanagi chip tape-out news late 2026. (4) Arm earnings showing continued AI revenue acceleration.

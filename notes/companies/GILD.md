# GILD -- Gilead Sciences

**Date:** 2026-03-19
**Price:** $141.29 | **Mkt Cap:** $175B | **Sector:** Biopharma (HIV, Oncology, Inflammation)

---

## Thesis

Gilead is a high-quality defensive biopharma with a dominant HIV franchise (~$20B+ revenue), strong FCF generation ($9.5B/yr), and improving oncology optionality. Lenacapavir (twice-yearly HIV injectable) is a paradigm-shifting product that secures the HIV franchise well past Biktarvy's 2033 patent cliff. The stock trades at ~15x forward earnings -- a discount to large-cap pharma peers -- with 4 product launches targeted in 2026 and no major LOE until 2036.

---

## Step 0: Situation & Context

**Recent news & catalysts:**
- FY2025 revenue $29.4B (+2.4% YoY), GAAP EPS $6.78 (vs $0.38 in FY2024 which had a one-time impairment)
- 2026 guidance: product sales $29.6-30.0B, non-GAAP EPS $8.45-8.85
- Arcellx acquisition ($7.8B) for anito-cel (BCMA CAR-T for myeloma), PDUFA Dec 2026
- Lenacapavir ARTISTRY Phase 3 data positive (bictegravir/lenacapavir combo)
- 4 launches targeted in 2026: Trodelvy 1L mTNBC, bictegravir/lenacapavir daily oral, anito-cel, bulevirtide (HDV)
- Yescarta label expansion approved (Feb 2026) for CNS lymphoma
- Analyst consensus: Buy, median PT $160, high $180

**Sector context:** Large-cap biopharma is defensively positioned amid macro uncertainty. HIV market ~$30B globally, growing. Oncology increasingly competitive but Gilead building a multi-modal portfolio (ADC + CAR-T + small molecule).

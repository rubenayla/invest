# Sociedad Quimica y Minera de Chile (SQM)

**Sector:** Basic Materials | **Industry:** Specialty Chemicals (Lithium, Iodine, Fertilizers)
**Price:** $74.66 | **Market Cap:** $21.3B | **EV:** $25.7B
**Analysis Date:** 2026-03-20

## Situation Summary

SQM has staged a dramatic recovery from its 2024 trough (~$29), more than doubling as lithium prices stabilized and the company returned to profitability with $588M net income in FY2025 vs a $404M loss in FY2024. The NovaAndino Litio JV with Codelco is now operational, producing 233kt LCE in 2025 (above guidance), with plans to expand ~30% over the coming years. Iodine has emerged as a critical profit pillar (42% of gross profit) with record prices. The stock now trades at $74.66 -- already up 154% from 52-week lows and only 13% below its 52-week high. The forward P/E of ~14x implies a near-tripling of net income to ~$1.6B, requiring significantly higher lithium prices.

## Variant Perception

- **Consensus view:** SQM is a recovery play on lithium, with most analysts at Hold/Buy expecting gradual improvement as lithium surplus narrows. The mean target is $76.50 -- essentially at current price. The market prices in a strong earnings recovery (forward P/E 14x vs trailing 36x) but is skeptical about sustained lithium price increases given supply overhang.
- **Our view:** The consensus may be underappreciating two factors: (1) iodine's structural strength -- SQM controls ~30% of global supply, prices are at record levels with tight supply from X-ray contrast media demand, and this is NOT a cyclical commodity the way lithium is; (2) the lithium surplus is narrowing faster than expected (S&P: from large surplus to 109kt in 2026, potential deficit by late 2026/2027), while SQM's brine cost base (<$5k/t) means it prints money at any lithium price above $12k/t. However, the stock has already run hard and much of this recovery is priced in.
- **Trigger:** Q1 2026 earnings (May 26) showing lithium price realization improvement + continued iodine strength. Alternatively, lithium spot price breaking above $18k/t sustainably.

## Inflection Point

**Yes -- SQM is past the trough but the easy money has been made.** The inflection from loss-making to $588M profit happened in FY2025. The stock has already re-rated from $29 to $75 on this recovery. The NEXT inflection would be lithium prices moving sustainably above $15-18k/t, which would drive the forward earnings the market is pricing in. Evidence is mixed: surplus is narrowing but still exists in 2026 (~109kt). A true deficit (and pricing power) may not arrive until late 2026/2027.

The iodine business is at its own inflection -- record prices, capacity expansion, structural demand growth from medical imaging -- but this is already partially reflected in the stock.

## Bull Case
- Lithium surplus flips to deficit in H2 2026, prices surge above $20k/t, SQM's low-cost base translates to $2B+ EBITDA
- Iodine sustains record pricing as supply remains structurally tight; new capacity adds incremental profit
- NovaAndino production ramp delivers 300kt+ LCE, making SQM the undisputed global #1
- DLE technology approval unlocks higher recovery rates at Salar de Atacama
- Energy storage demand (grid batteries) creates a second structural demand driver beyond EVs

## Bear Case
- Lithium prices stall at $10-12k/t as Chinese supply restarts, surplus persists through 2027
- Forward P/E of 14x already bakes in aggressive earnings recovery that doesn't materialize
- Codelco JV state take squeezes shareholder economics -- SQM keeps only 30-40% of operating profit
- Iodine prices mean-revert from record levels as competitors expand
- Chile political risk: royalty/tax regime changes further erode margins
- Stock has already run 154% from lows -- limited upside unless lithium breaks out

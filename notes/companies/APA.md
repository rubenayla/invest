## Risks

### 1. Oil Price Sensitivity (HIGH)
- No refining to offset production volatility
- Pure bet on oil/gas prices
- Revenue down 13.6% YoY from lower prices

### 2. Production Decline (MEDIUM-HIGH)
- Shale wells decline 60-80% in first year
- Need continuous drilling to maintain production
- Capex treadmill (drill just to stay flat)

### 3. Commodity Volatility (HIGH)
- Natural gas prices very volatile
- Oil price swings directly hit earnings
- No pricing power

### 4. Energy Transition Risk (MEDIUM)
- Long-term demand uncertainty
- ESG investors avoid pure E&P
- Asset stranding risk (can't sell reserves)

### 5. Debt Burden (MEDIUM)
- 68% debt-to-equity
- Need $60+ oil to comfortably service debt
- Refinancing risk

### 6. Geological Risk (LOW-MEDIUM)
- Dry holes, lower production than expected
- Reservoir performance uncertainty

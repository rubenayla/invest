# Core Scientific (CORZ)

**Sector**: Technology (AI/HPC Infrastructure, formerly Bitcoin Mining)
**Verdict**: **WATCH — Potential Deep Value in BTC-to-AI Pivot**

## Investment Thesis

Core Scientific is the value play in the BTC miner-to-AI datacenter pivot. It trades at a significant discount to peers despite having the largest contracted revenue in the sector.

**Why it's interesting:**
- **Valuation gap**: ~$4M/MW vs ~$7M/MW peer average (IREN, WULF). Closing that gap = ~75% upside on asset value alone.
- **$8.7B CoreWeave contract** (12-year take-or-pay): Provides revenue visibility rare in this sector.
- **1.2 GW power portfolio**: Among the largest in the miner cohort. ~400 MW allocated to AI/HPC.
- **Already billing**: ~200 MW in AI revenue, unlike Tier 2 peers (HUT, RIOT) that are still pre-revenue on AI.

## Bull Case

1. Trades at deep discount to peers on EV/MW basis
2. CoreWeave contract is investment-grade quality (now public post-IPO)
3. Infrastructure-in-place advantage: pre-energized sites save 5-7 year interconnect timelines
4. Revenue mix shifting from volatile BTC mining to predictable long-term leases
5. Potential for additional hyperscaler contracts on remaining capacity

## Bear Case

1. **Revenue gap**: FY2025 revenue fell 38% during mining-to-AI transition
2. **Single customer risk**: CoreWeave is essentially the only AI tenant
3. **CoreWeave risk**: CRWV itself is highly levered and not yet profitable — if CoreWeave struggles, the contract is at risk
4. **Execution**: Running Tier III/IV datacenters with 99.99% uptime SLAs is fundamentally different from mining containers
5. **Capital needs**: AI-ready buildout costs $8-11M/MW, funded largely by debt
6. **Already re-rated**: Emerged from bankruptcy in 2024, stock has recovered significantly

## Key Metrics to Track

| Metric | What to watch |
|--------|--------------|
| MW billing | How fast AI revenue ramps (target: 400 MW by mid-2026) |
| EV/MW vs peers | Gap closing = thesis working |
| CoreWeave financial health | CRWV earnings, cash burn, contract amendments |
| New contracts | Additional hyperscaler leases beyond CoreWeave |
| Mining revenue decline | Expected, but pace matters for cash flow bridge |

## Scenario Analysis

| Scenario | Prob | Outcome |

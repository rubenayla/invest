# Lasertec Corporation (6920.T)

**Sector:** Technology | **Industry:** Semiconductor Equipment & Materials
**Price:** 36,020 JPY | **Market Cap:** 3.25T JPY (~$21B USD)
**Analysis Date:** 2026-03-18

## Situation Summary

Lasertec is the global monopoly supplier of EUV photomask inspection equipment, a critical bottleneck tool for advanced semiconductor manufacturing. The stock has surged +112% in the last 6 months and +163% over the past year, recovering spectacularly from the Scorpion Capital short attack (June 2024) and a severe order trough. FY2025 (ended June 2025) was a blockbuster year with revenue of 251.5B JPY (+18% YoY) and operating income of 122.8B JPY (+51% YoY). However, the company has guided for a significant down year in FY2026: revenue 200B JPY (-20% YoY), net income 60B JPY (-29% YoY), reflecting order weakness from FY2025 flowing through. Q1 FY2026 beat expectations massively (+35% EPS surprise, +27% revenue surprise), and order momentum appears to be recovering — triggering the March 2026 rally. The next earnings report is expected around April 28-May 5, 2026.

## Variant Perception

- **Consensus view:** Mixed. 15 analysts covering: recommendation skews "buy" but with a mean target of 35,900 JPY (basically flat from current price). The market recognizes the monopoly position but is pricing in the FY2026 earnings trough and uncertainty about the order recovery timeline. The Scorpion Capital fraud allegations (June 2024) have been largely dismissed by the market given subsequent strong results, but created lingering skepticism. The consensus is that Lasertec is a great company going through a cyclical down year, with recovery starting in CY2026.

- **Our view:** The stock has already priced in the recovery and then some. At 36,020 JPY, it trades at 54x FY2026 guided EPS (665.75 JPY) during what management calls a trough year. Even on trailing peak earnings (936 EPS), it trades at 38.5x. The Q1 beat and order momentum have been aggressively front-run — the stock is up 112% in 6 months. For the current price to be justified, you need FY2027 EPS of ~900+ JPY AND the market to sustain a 40x PE, which assumes a full return to peak earnings AND premium valuation. This is plausible but already priced in. There is no variant perception — the monopoly story is well-understood, and the order recovery is consensus.

- **Trigger:** Q3 FY2026 results (late April 2026) showing order intake acceleration or guidance raise would validate the recovery thesis. ZEISS entry into EUV mask inspection (potential competitive threat) could be a negative trigger in 2027+.

## Inflection Point

**Negative inflection (post-peak cyclical decline):** FY2025 was likely the cyclical peak. Orders fell to 105.2B JPY and FY2026 guided revenue is -20%. The company is entering the down phase of its cycle, though Q1 FY2026 beat and recent order momentum suggest the trough may be shallower than feared.

**Potential positive inflection (order recovery):** If CY2026 brings an EUV investment cycle (driven by Intel 18A/20A ramp, TSMC N2, Samsung gate-all-around), Lasertec's order book could inflect higher in H2 FY2026 or FY2027. However, the stock has already priced this in with its 112% run in 6 months.

## Bull Case

- EUV mask inspection is a structural monopoly with 100% market share and extreme switching costs
- Order recovery in CY2026 drives FY2027 revenue back to or above FY2025 levels (250B+ JPY)
- New products (ACTIS A300, ABICS E320, SICA108 SiC wafer) expand TAM beyond traditional mask inspection
- High Numerical Aperture (High-NA) EUV from ASML creates incremental demand for next-gen inspection tools
- Near-zero debt and massive FCF generation fund buybacks or special dividends

## Bear Case

- **Valuation is extreme:** 54x FY2026E EPS, 12.9x P/S, 14.3x P/B — priced for perfection in a down year
- **Cyclical trough may deepen:** If EUV capex delays extend (Intel restructuring, Samsung yield issues), FY2026 could miss even conservative guidance
- **ZEISS competitive entry:** ZEISS developing EUV mask inspection tools would be the first real competitive threat; even announcement could compress the monopoly premium
- **Scorpion Capital overhang:** While the market has dismissed the fraud allegations, the accounting questions (high inventory levels, Innovation Park concerns) linger; any new evidence or regulatory inquiry would devastate the stock
- **Concentration risk:** ~26 total installed EUV inspection units across 3 customers — extreme customer concentration

## Verdict

**PASS** — Conviction: HIGH

Lasertec is a genuinely exceptional business — a near-monopoly in a critical semiconductor bottleneck with world-class margins (49% operating), zero debt, and a fortress balance sheet. Quality score of 20/25 reflects this. However, the stock at 36,020 JPY is pricing in a full recovery that hasn't happened yet. At 54x guided FY2026 EPS during a -20% revenue year, the valuation demands perfection. The expected value from our scenario analysis is -21.6%, driven by the asymmetry between limited upside (+19% bull case) and severe downside (-54% to -69% bear cases). The stock has already run 112% in 6 months, front-running the order recovery catalyst.

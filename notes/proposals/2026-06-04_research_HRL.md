  ($1.43–$1.51) reaffirmed.

  turkey business (a $61M one-off loss, an operating improvement). Branded/value-added mix improving.
- **Strong income floor.** A-rated, ~1.7x net leverage, and the **Hormel Foundation owns ~47%** —
  a near-religious dividend backstop. You're paid ~5% to wait.

## Why it's a starter, not a back-up-the-truck

- **Earnings have been flat for four years** (revenue ~$12B FY2022–FY2025; adj EPS $1.82→$1.37).
  The premium multiple may be permanently gone; 15x could be fair, not cheap.
- **FY2025 FCF ($0.53B) did not cover the dividend ($0.63B)** — first uncovered year. A second
  would turn the "streak" into a balance-sheet drain.
- **The cash-flow DCFs land near ~$11** (roughly half today's price) on a no-growth terminal — the
  real bear tail, represented by the 35% bear weight.

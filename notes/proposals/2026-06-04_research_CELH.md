# Research Proposal — Celsius Holdings (CELH)

**Date:** 2026-06-04
**Price:** $30.01 (2026-06-03 close) | **Market Cap:** ~$7.7B
**Verdict:** BUY (starter / scale-in) — MEDIUM conviction
**Full note:** `notes/companies/CELH.md`

## Why this clears the "look now" bar

1. **Deep de-rating (~60% off 2024 highs)** to $30, while the business roughly *doubled* via the Alani Nu acquisition (closed Apr 2025). The multiple compressed to a value level (~2.6x EV/sales, ~15–21x forward EPS) on a portfolio that is now the **#2 US energy player at ~20.9% category dollar share**.

2. **Fresh catalyst that refutes the core bear thesis (Q1 2026, reported May 7 2026):**
   - Revenue **$782.6M, +138% YoY**; **EPS $0.41 vs $0.30 consensus (+37% beat)**.
   - **CELSIUS brand +6% organic** — the legacy core is growing again, not melting (the bear's central premise).
   - **Alani Nu ~$368M, ~100% YoY**; combined portfolio ~21% category share and ~45% of zero-sugar category growth.

3. **Unusual sell-side configuration:** mean target **$61.4** (n=20, "buy"), and the **entire range — low $41, high $85 — sits above the current $30**. When even the most bearish published target is ~37% above the quote, the de-rating likely overshot.

## Quantified risk/reward

| Scenario | Prob | Fair Value | vs $30 |
|----------|------|-----------|--------|
| Bull (~18% compound, margins to ~14%) | 30% | ~$59 | +97% |
| Base (~10% growth, margin ~12.5%) | 45% | ~$37 | +23% |
| Bear (core re-stalls, destock + promo wars) | 25% | ~$15 | −50% |

**Probability-weighted ≈ $38 (~+27%)** over ~2 years. Asymmetry is positive but not extreme — the −50% bear is a live outcome if organic growth re-stalls.

## Entry / exit

- **Entry:** ~$30 starter; add toward **$25–26** (near the $21 multi-year low).
- **Thesis-break:** **~$21** price; or fundamentally — CELSIUS-brand organic growth negative two quarters running, combined category share below ~18%, or gross margin below ~44% (promo-war signal).
- **What changes the mind:** a second PepsiCo inventory destock, Alani scanner growth decelerating below ~30% as it laps the deal, or Monster/Red Bull retaking zero-sugar shelf.

## Honest caveat

This is MEDIUM conviction, not high. The headline +138% is mostly *acquired* growth; the thesis rests on two unproven things holding — the core staying positive and Alani decelerating *gracefully* rather than rolling over. Size accordingly (starter, scale-in), don't back up the truck at $30.

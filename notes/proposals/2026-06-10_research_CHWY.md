## The setup in one paragraph
Profitable pet e-commerce leader, **~14.5x EV/FCF, ~10x forward P/E, net-cash balance sheet, FCF +24% YoY** ($562M, four-year ramp $119→343→452→562M on only ~6% revenue growth — a genuine margin-expansion story via pharmacy/private-label/ads/automation). The de-rate is half fundamental (growth decelerated from teens to mid-single-digits as the COVID pet-adoption wave reversed; active customers stalled ~20M) and half technical (**BC Partners** sponsor selling into a tiny **~62M-share float** — a mechanical lid that clears only when the sponsor is out). No dividend, so it's a pure appreciation thesis.

## What to watch in the print (the whole thesis hinges here)

- **NSPAC** (net sales per active customer) and **adjusted EBITDA margin** — must keep rising for the margin thesis.
- **FY guide** and any color on **BC Partners' remaining stake** (overhang nearing its end = re-rate fuel).

# Research Proposal: DocuSign (DOCU) — first look

**Date:** 2026-06-18 | **Price:** $42.58 (at 52-week low, range $40.16–$86.65)
**Verdict:** WATCH (lean BUY LOW under ~$38) | **Conviction:** LOW–MEDIUM | **Scenario EV: ~+19%**

## One-paragraph thesis

DocuSign sits at its 52-week low and screens as deep value — **~13% headline FCF yield, ~6x EV/FCF, net cash** — but the cheapness is **half-illusion**: stock-based comp runs **$622M/yr (~19% of revenue)**, so on GAAP it's **~26x earnings** and on SBC-adjusted owner-earnings only a **~6–7% yield** — fairly valued for an ~8% grower, not a bargain. What makes it worth watching is a **verified inflection the bears ignore**: dollar net retention has risen for **six consecutive quarters to 102%**, and the **IAM platform exploded from 2.3% → 10.8% of ARR in one year** ($350M+, guided to $600M+/18% next year). The core e-signature business has decelerated to ~4–6% billings growth (the source of the de-rating), but if IAM converts its ARR-mix surge into reported total-growth re-acceleration, the stock re-rates off a trough. You're paid a fair coupon to wait via a $1.4B buyback that more than offsets dilution, and net cash cushions the downside.

## Why flag it now

- **Fresh, never-covered name** at its **52-week low** — a setup the owner hunts.
- **Notable, verified catalyst:** NRR up 6 straight quarters + IAM mix shift accelerating (not a vanity metric).
- **Asymmetry is acceptable, not fat:** ~+19% EV with a cushioned left tail (net cash, real cash generation, buyback). This is a WATCH, not a buy-now.

## The kill (why it's only WATCH)

- **SBC at 19% of revenue** makes the FCF-cheapness an optical illusion; on GAAP it's 26x for single-digit growth.
- Billings decelerating to ~4–6% and full-year guidance *cut*; core e-sign is commoditizing (Adobe, Dropbox Sign, free tools).
- Four-year value trap — cheap has not been a catalyst since the 2021 peak. The entire upside leans on IAM converting promise into reported growth, which guidance promises but billings don't yet show.

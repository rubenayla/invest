# Research Proposal — Zoetis (ZTS), 2026-06-04

**Grade:** B (solid starter idea, real asymmetry) · **Call:** BUY scale-in · **EV ≈ +56%**

**Hook:** Global #1 animal-health franchise de-rated to a decade-low ~11x after one bad quarter.

## Why look now
ZTS is **$77.59, down ~55%** from its early-2025 high and sitting on its 52-week low after a ~25% single-day drop on the **May 7, 2026 Q1 print + guidance cut**. The market has repriced the world's largest animal-health company from a ~30–35x premium compounder to **~11x forward earnings** (~10.5x on Street EPS), with a 2.78% growing dividend.

Crucially, the franchise economics are **undamaged** — 72% gross margin, 37% operating margin, 68% ROE — and the business is **decelerating, not declining**: revenue still +2–5% organic, livestock +12%, EPS still grew +3% in Q1. The selloff is a *pricing-power scare* concentrated in US companion animal (−11%), where dermatology (Apoquel/Cytopoint −11%) and parasiticides (Simparica −1%) face simultaneous new competition (Merck Numelvi, Elanco Zenrelia & Credelio Quattro).

## The bet
The de-rating overshoots a cyclical/competitive air-pocket. A category leader still growing, with intact moat economics, at ~11x is mispriced; even a partial re-rate to a still-discounted 16–20x on stabilized ~$7–8 EPS is +60% to +120%.

## The risk (why B, not A)
The bear case is **genuinely structural, not sentiment**: for the first time well-funded rivals attack Zoetis's two biggest cash franchises at once, US companion (its highest-margin engine) is shrinking, and deceleration has run four straight quarters. If derm/parasiticide pricing power is permanently impaired, ~11x is the new normal, not a bargain. Hence **scale-in, not full size**.

## Scenario summary (≈3y, to FY28)
| Scenario | Prob | Price | Return |
|----------|------|-------|--------|
| Bull (re-rate, reaccelerate) | 30% | ~$170 | +119% |
| Base (decel moderates, partial re-rate) | 40% | ~$124 | +60% |
| Bear (structural erosion) | 20% | ~$77 | −1% |
| Severe bear (EPS declines) | 10% | ~$60 | −23% |

**Prob-weighted EV ≈ +56%.** Downside ~−23% worst realistic case vs +60–120% base/bull.

## Action
- **Entry:** scale in from ~$77; add toward ~$68–70 (≈10x fwd, >3% yield).
- **Thesis-break:** below ~$60 *with* worsening US companion declines, gross-margin compression, or a second FY26 EPS guidance cut below ~$6.50.

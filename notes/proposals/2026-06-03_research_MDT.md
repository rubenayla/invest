## One-line thesis
The exact trigger our March WATCH was waiting for — Q4 FY26 confirming reacceleration with FY27
organic guidance **above 5%** — fired this morning to the upside (FY27 organic **+6.75% to +7.25%**),
yet the stock is **11% cheaper than in March ($87 → $77)** and one day off a 52-week low. Same/better
thesis, lower price, paid 3.7% to wait.

## Why now (the mispricing)
- **The open question got answered favorably.** March note (WATCH, +8.6% EV) hinged on whether
  mid-single-digit organic growth was real. FY26 printed **+5.8% organic — best in 10 years** — and
  management guided **FY27 to +6.75–7.25% organic / +6.7–8.5% non-GAAP EPS ($5.90–$6.00)**. That's
  *above* the March base case's 5% assumption.
- **Price went the wrong way.** $87 (March) → $73.75 low (2026-06-02) → $77.46 (+5% on today's print).
  Forward P/E is now **~13.0x** on the $5.95 FY27 midpoint vs. 14.3x in March — cheaper on better growth.
- **Carry + cushion.** 3.7% dividend yield (48th consecutive annual raise), price one day off the
  52-week low, with just-printed beat-and-raise momentum.

## The honest bear case (why it's BUY-small, not high-conviction)
- **No margin leverage.** FY27 EPS growth (6.7–8.5%) ≈ revenue growth (~7%): tariffs (~$300M+),
  interest, tax, and full consolidation of the lower-margin Diabetes business eat the operating leverage.
  This is a *growth + re-rating* story, not a margin story.
- **Token dividend hike (+1.4%)** and **~90% FCF payout** → slow dividend growth, thin buyback room
  until FCF/margins inflect.
- **Diabetes was a partial IPO (priced $20, below the $25–28 range) and is retained/consolidated** —
  not the clean spin the March note implied; the de-overhang catalyst is slower than hoped.
- **$28B gross / ~$20B net debt** (2.1x EBITDA) limits flexibility.

## What flips it more bullish
Gross margin back above 66% / tariff offsets landing; Hugo winning general-surgery or gynecology
indications in CY2026; a faster Diabetes separation. Any one pushes fair value toward the bull case.

## Action
Owner-facing call: initiate or add a **small** position ≤ $78, add < $74, exit on a sustained
close < $70 or two quarters of sub-4% organic growth. Not a table-pounder — a high-quality medtech
franchise re-rated to ~13x just as its growth reaccelerated, paying 3.7% while the thesis plays out.

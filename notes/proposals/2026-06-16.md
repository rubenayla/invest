## ADBE (Adobe) — BUY reaffirmed, EV widened to ~+70%. Fresh actionable bargain at $206.

**One-line:** A top-conviction BUY printed a beat-and-raise that *confirmed* the bull thesis on 2026-06-11, yet the stock fell ~13% to **$206.36** on a CFO-poaching governance scare — leaving it cheaper than its own add zone at **~8.5x forward earnings**.

### Why this clears the bar
ADBE is the highest-EV BUY in the universe and its decisive catalyst (Q2 FY2026 earnings) just resolved *in favour of the thesis*, but the note was last updated pre-earnings (2026-06-10). The print and the post-print selloff together create a clean entry: business confirmed, price down.

### The Q2 FY2026 print (verified vs the 8-K / press release)
- **Record revenue $6.62B (+13% YoY)** — beat ~$6.45B consensus and the $6.43-6.48B company guide.
- **Non-GAAP EPS $5.96 (+18% YoY)** — above the $5.85-5.90 guide.
- **Total ARR $27.10B**; **AI-first ARR tripled YoY, now >$500M**; **Firefly ARR approaching $300M (~+50% QoQ)**; Creative freemium MAU >90M (+70% YoY).
- Creative+marketing subs **+13% to $4.54B**; business/consumer subs **+16% to $1.85B** — **no Creative Cloud seat erosion**.
- **FY26 guidance raised**: revenue $26.5-26.6B, non-GAAP EPS **$24.35-24.45**.
- **None of the three thesis-break conditions fired.** The "AI is a Kodak moment for Adobe" bear story took another empirical hit.

### Why it fell anyway (the opportunity)
CFO **Dan Durn announced his exit effective 2026-06-15 to become CFO of Marvell** — a lateral poaching for a bigger seat, not a departure for cause or any accounting issue. On a high-expectations AI name it became a *second* C-suite vacancy (alongside the ongoing CEO search) and the market sold the headline (~−7% on the day). **Steve Day, a 20-year Adobe finance veteran, is interim CFO**, reporting to Chair/CEO Narayen. This is a temporary sentiment/governance overhang, not a fundamental crack.

~8.5x the raised FY26 EPS guide ($24.40), ~12% FCF yield — *below* the note's prior "historical low ex-2008" of 9.7x and below its own stale $220-240 add zone.

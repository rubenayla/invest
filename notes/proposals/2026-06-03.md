The market is discounting future Evernorth earnings erosion that Q1's rebate-free print suggests is overstated: the new model trades opaque rebate margin for stickier, transparent fee revenue, and Q1 shows the profit pool holding while the segment still grows. Reform largely codifies a shift Cigna already led. That is the crux of the disagreement — and it's a real, bounded risk, not a thesis-killer.

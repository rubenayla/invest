### The live bear case (why it's cheap, honestly)
The 2026 PBM-reform law (Consolidated Appropriations Act, signed **2026-02-03**) + the FTC–Express Scripts settlement (**2026-02-04**) mandate 100% rebate pass-through and Medicare Part D fee-only economics — but these **phase in 2028–2029**. The market is discounting future Evernorth earnings erosion that Q1's rebate-free print suggests is overstated: the new model trades opaque rebate margin for stickier, transparent fee revenue, and Q1 shows the profit pool holding while the segment still grows. Reform largely codifies a shift Cigna already led. That is the crux of the disagreement — and it's a real, bounded risk, not a thesis-killer.

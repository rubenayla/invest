## Why this clears the "look now" bar

Quality medtech franchise at a **52-week-low valuation with a clearly transitory catalyst** — the exact de-rated-quality profile of the names recently added to the watchlist (MRK, ZTS, MA, PEP, GIS).

- **#2 global medical-imaging franchise** (CT/MRI/molecular/ultrasound/contrast) behind Siemens Healthineers, with a moaty installed base (~40–45% recurring service/consumables revenue), the industry's **largest FDA-cleared AI device portfolio**, ROE ~19%, gross margin ~39%.
- Trading at **~12.7x forward adj EPS**, just **5% above its 52-week low ($58.75)** and **−31% from its high ($89.77)**. Versus its own 14–18x band and medtech peers at 18–22x.
- The de-rating is a **margin** story, not a demand story: Q1'26 revenue **+2.9% organic / +7.4% reported**, EBIT margin still **expanding** YoY. Two guidance trims (FY26 adj EPS $4.95–5.15 → $4.80–5.00) driven by **~$250M transitory input-cost inflation** (memory chips $100M, freight $100M, metals $50M) + tariffs — with price/cost actions guided to offset >half and inflect positive in **2027**.
- Guidance set conservatively: **no** IEEPA tariff-refund credit assumed, cautious-China baked in.
- **Downside bounded:** 5% to the 52-week low, defensive beta 0.87, ~$1.5B FCF, healthcare end-market.
- **Street:** mean target $79.72 (+28%); the *low* target $65 is above today's price; 18 analysts Buy.

## The bear case (why it's MEDIUM not HIGH)

- **Serial cutter** — two trims in a row; a *third* would confirm the market's "management keeps walking it down" read and there'd be no re-rate.
- **China** soft (anti-corruption, stimulus delays, United Imaging/Mindray share gains) — if it turns to outright revenue *decline* the thesis breaks.
- **Leverage** ~2.4x net debt / EBITDA, token 0.2% dividend → no income support.
- Only **3–4% organic growth** — not a fast compounder; the case rests on multiple re-rating + margin normalization, not unit growth.

## Action

- **Entry:** starter ~$62; scale-in $55–58.
- **Thesis-break:** sustained close <$52 *on* a third cut + China revenue decline.
- **Re-rates on:** one clean (held/raised) quarter, margin inflecting up in 2027, China stabilizing, deleveraging through ~2x.

# Proposal: APTV (Aptiv) — first look, post-Versigent-spin RemainCo at ~9x forward

**Date:** 2026-06-23 · **Price:** $61.42 · **Rating:** BUY LOW (lean) · **EV ≈ +25%**

## Why look now
Aptiv screens as "−31% off 52-week high," but that is **mostly mechanical**: on 2026-04-01 it spun off
its wiring-harness business as **Versigent (VGNT)**, distributing 1 VGNT per 3 APTV shares. Pre-spin
~$82–85 (Feb) → ~$62 on distribution → ~$54 trough (mid-May) → $61 now. The investable question is
whether the **higher-margin RemainCo** (connectors, high-voltage electrification, ADAS/software) is
cheap at ~9x forward earnings. It plausibly is.

## The setup
- Clean RemainCo: ~$13.6B revenue (2027E), ~10% operating margin, EPS $6.15 (2026E) → $6.82 (2027E, +11%).
- $61.42 = ~10x 2026 / ~9x 2027 earnings; P/B ~1.4x; ~8% FCF yield; analyst mean target $78 (+27%), strong-buy.
- Classic "keep the brains, spin the harness" — RemainCo deserves a higher multiple than the old blended co.

## Why not higher conviction
Leveraged auto-cyclical (net debt ~2x EBITDA) in a sector that earns its low multiple; 2027 revenue
actually dips vs 2026; ADAS/software contested by Mobileye/Nvidia/Qualcomm. "9x forward" could be fair,
not cheap — value-trap risk is real. Posture: **buy the post-spin weakness (~$54–56), don't chase $61+.**

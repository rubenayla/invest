**Full note:** `notes/companies/GIS.md`

## Why look now

General Mills is at **$32 — its 2018 price and −56% from the 2022 peak (~$73)** — a genuine
decade-low for a 126-year dividend payer now yielding **~7.4%, covered 1.7x by FCF**. The market is
pricing terminal decline; the evidence says fiscal-2026 is a *deliberate reset/investment trough*.

- FY2026 guidance (reaffirmed Mar-2026): organic sales −1.5/−2%, **adjusted EPS −16/−20% c.c.** →
  ~$3.45 trough. Stock = **~9x trough earnings, ~7.5x true EV/EBITDA** (net debt $14.5B; yfinance's
  $19.8B EV understates debt — real EV ≈ $31.7B).
- The down-year is self-inflicted: management gave pricing back to repair volume and lapped
  divestitures. Q3 FY26 still **held/gained pound share in 7 of 10 top US categories**.
- Guides Q4 FY26 to an organic-sales **step-up + return to earnings growth**, and FY27 to improved
  organic sales "with price-investment work behind it."

## Asymmetry

| Scenario | Total return (2–3y) |
|---|---|
| Bull (volume repair sticks, $4.10 EPS @14x) | ~+90% |
| Base ($3.70 EPS @12x) | ~+50% |
| Bear (erosion continues, $3.00 EPS @9.5x, div frozen) | ~+5% |

7.4% yield + trough multiple cushion the downside to roughly flat; normalization pays +50–90%.

## Risks (why B, not A)

- **$14.5B net debt / ~3.5x leverage** — elevated for a declining staple; thin equity cushion.
- Secular center-store headwinds (private label + GLP-1); pet/Blue Buffalo growth engine stumbling.
- Net income down **3 straight years**; "reset year" could become reset years — classic KHC-style
  value-trap risk. No near-term hard catalyst.

## The tell to watch

**Late-June 2026: GIS Q4 FY26 print.** Organic-sales step-up + FY27 "return to growth" intact →
thesis on track, add. No step-up *and* an FY27 cut → value-trap confirmation, stand down. A break
below ~$28 on fundamentals (not market beta), or a dividend cut, breaks the thesis.

## Suggested action

Starter/scale-in now at ~$32; size up on the late-June Q4 confirmation or on weakness into the
high-$20s. Run `/size GIS` for Kelly sizing before committing capital.

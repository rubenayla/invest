# Bitcoin Miners as AI Datacenter Infrastructure Thesis
*Analysis Date: 2026-03-07*
*Last Updated: 2026-03-07*

---

## Executive Summary

**Thesis**: Bitcoin miners are pivoting en masse to AI/HPC datacenter infrastructure. They possess the three scarcest resources in AI: power contracts, grid interconnections, and land. The 2024 halving crushed mining economics (hashprice at record lows ~$34/PH/s), making the pivot existential. Hyperscalers (Google, Microsoft, AWS, Anthropic) are validating the thesis with multi-billion-dollar take-or-pay contracts.

**Verdict**: **CAUTIOUSLY BULLISH -- Stock Selection Critical**

The thesis is real. The question is not *whether* miners pivot, but *which ones execute*. The sector has bifurcated: Tier 1 players with signed mega-contracts are re-rating as infrastructure companies, while laggards remain mining-dependent and face insolvency risk. The "easy" re-rating has happened for leaders (IREN up 9x, HUT up 15x from lows). Value may remain in CORZ ($4M/MW vs $7M/MW peer average) and selective Tier 2 plays.

## 3. Bull Case

1. **Infrastructure arbitrage**: Pre-energized sites save 5-7 years of utility interconnect timelines. This is the real moat.

2. **Hyperscaler validation**: Google (HUT, WULF), Microsoft ($9.7B IREN), AWS (CIFR), Anthropic (HUT) signing take-or-pay contracts with investment-grade counterparties.

3. **Non-dilutive financing**: Google/Microsoft credit guarantees enable 85% LTC project financing.

4. **Revenue quality upgrade**: Volatile BTC mining revenue --> 10-25 year fixed-rate leases with Fortune 10 customers.

5. **GPU scarcity**: NVIDIA Blackwell sold out through mid-2026. Early allocators (IREN, CoreWeave) have locked-in revenue.

6. **Valuation gap**: CORZ trades at $4M/MW vs $7M/MW peer average -- potential 75% upside to peer valuation alone.

---

## 4. Bear Case

1. **Mining ≠ datacenter operations**: Running mining containers vs. Tier III/IV with 99.99% uptime SLAs are fundamentally different businesses. Cooling, redundancy, networking, security are orders of magnitude more complex.

2. **Execution risk**: Multi-billion-dollar construction projects. Transformer/switchgear lead times are bottleneck. CoreWeave already faces lawsuit over overstated capacity claims.

3. **Capital intensity**: AI-ready facilities cost $8M-$11M/MW (and rising). Most miners can't fund from cash flow.

4. **Customer concentration**: IREN ~100% Microsoft, HUT ~100% Anthropic/Google. Single counterparty risk.

5. **Hyperscaler competition**: MSFT, GOOG, AMZN, META all building their own datacenters at massive scale. Miners are intermediaries that could be squeezed or bypassed.

6. **Revenue gap**: 12-24 month transition period where mining revenue declines before AI revenue ramps. CORZ revenue fell 38% in FY2025 during transition.

7. **Already priced in for leaders**: IREN up 9x, HUT up 15x from lows. The "pivot premium" may be fully reflected.

8. **Rising electricity costs**: Wholesale prices projected ~$51/MWh in 2026 (+8.5%), compressing all margins.

## 6. Actionable Ideas

### Highest Conviction

- **CORZ** (Core Scientific): $4M/MW vs $7M/MW peer average. If it trades at peer valuation = ~75% upside. $8.7B contracted revenue with CoreWeave. The value play in the sector.

### Watch List

- **WULF** (TeraWulf): Most contracted cash flow visibility ($12.8B). Google credit enhancement reduces financing risk. But stock has already re-rated significantly.
- **IREN**: Scale leader but at ~$14B mkt cap, Microsoft dependency is the key risk. Premium valuation, needs continued execution.

### Avoid for Now

- **MARA**: Late mover, lost early positioning. Starwood JV is interesting but unproven.
- **BITF**: Too small, too early. Phase 1 only 50 MW.
- **RIOT**: Slowest pivot, still 89% mining revenue. Unless they announce a mega-lease.

### Alternative Plays

- Instead of picking individual miners, consider the **picks-and-shovels** approach:
  - **NVDA**: GPU supplier to all of them
  - **VST/CEG**: Power companies benefiting from datacenter demand (see [power thesis](power-thesis.md))

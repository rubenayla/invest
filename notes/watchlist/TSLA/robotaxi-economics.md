# Robotaxi economics (TSLA)

## Update: remove intraday charging
1. Charging time: you're right -- remove it from the day

A Model 3 / Model Y Long Range has:

~500-600 km real-world range

Even more with LFP daily cycling tolerance

From the revised utilization we had:

~490 km/day

That fits within one full daily cycle.

So:

No intraday charging

Charging happens overnight, off-peak

No revenue time lost

Remove charging downtime entirely from operating hours.

2. Updated operating window (clean)

You proposed:

06:00 -> 00:00 usable

That's 18 hours/day.

Now subtract only:

cleaning / repositioning / idle gaps

Be conservative but realistic:

18 - 2 ~= 16 h/day productive driving

3. Updated utilization (final)

Use a conservative average speed = 35 km/h.

16 h/day x 35 km/h = 560 km/day

Annualized:

560 x 365 ~= 204,000 km/year

Round down to avoid fantasy:

200,000 km/year per robotaxi

This is now a clean, defensible number.

4. Rider pricing: adjust to your assumption

You said:

"People will probably pay about the same as owning a car for the benefit of not having to drive."

That's reasonable.

Earlier we used EUR 0.30/km to kill ownership.
Now we allow price parity, not discount.

Use:

EUR 0.45 / km

This is:

~EUR 9,000/year for 20,000 km personal use

Matches real ownership cost

Still attractive due to zero hassle

5. Gross revenue per vehicle

200,000 km x EUR 0.45 = EUR 90,000 / year

This is not aggressive at this utilization.

6. Operating costs (high-utilization, realistic)

At 200,000 km/year:

Electricity: EUR 0.05/km -> EUR 10,000

Maintenance + tires: EUR 0.04/km -> EUR 8,000

Insurance, cleaning, misc: EUR 2,000

Total:

~= EUR 20,000 / year

7. Capital return requirement (your P/E 10 framing)

Vehicle cost:

EUR 35,000

Required return (10%):

EUR 3,500 / year

Total required cash out:

EUR 20,000 + EUR 3,500 = EUR 23,500

8. Economic surplus per vehicle

EUR 90,000 - EUR 23,500 ~= EUR 66,500 / year

This is pure surplus.

This is the number that changes everything.

So Tesla either:

Tesla capture ~= EUR 40,000 per vehicle per year
